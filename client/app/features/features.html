Features

BetaFish is one smart fish. Add it to your app and tell it when and where to collect feedback, otherwise it will stay out of site.

Get more detail from your feedback

BetaFish provides all the detail you need to properly diagnose feedback. You can also feed custom user data to BetaFish via our javascript API, and we’ll surface those user details in the feedback tickets when they get submitted.

3 ways to collect feedback

  1. Open feedback for all visitors

    You can make BetaFish available for collecting feedback to all visitors who access your product. Users may select from 4 different categories by hovering over the fishbowl.

    BetaFish will take a screenshot and keep track of the browser, OS, time and url path the feedback was given on.

  2. Feedback partners only

    Hide BetaFish by default and show it only to users you wish to receive feedback from by inviting them to become your feedback partners.

  3. Checkpoint-prompted feedback

    Set checkpoints for BetaFish to appear at key moments in your product’s experience. Checkpoints can be set to trigger off when a visitor goes to a certain url or when you call our javascript API.

    Create surveys to get the feedback you need, on the features and flows you want to learn about.
