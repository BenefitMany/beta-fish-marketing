USABILITY TESTING from within YOUR Web APP

Beta Fish is a widget that tests your app's user experience

  • Simple setup by pasting a script tag to your app

  • Users can send comments by category from inside your app

  • Screenshots are automatically included with the feedback

  • We report your user's browser, OS and screen resolution

  • You can ask users to test specific features and flows

  • Feedback tickets are easily portable to your project management app

  • Beta Fish is ideal for staging or beta environments

Beta Fish sits at the bottom of your app to gather user feedback

Users can leave feedback on their own
