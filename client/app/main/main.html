A better feedback widget

BetaFish appears on the corner of your web app to get feedback at the right times, from the right people.

  • Simple setup by pasting a script tag to your app

  • Users can send comments by category from inside your app

  • Screenshots are automatically included with the feedback

  • Detailed feedback includes browser, OS and screen resolution

  • Send user surveys at the right moments by creating checkpoints

  • Control who gets to see it and when

  • Tickets are portable to project management apps
